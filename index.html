Creator: Adam Phillips

Brackenwood is the name of the award-winning series of animations. It currently includes a continuing series of short Flash animations about the inhabitants of a small fictional forest planet of the same name. There are currently six official Brackenwood animations, with one supplemental music video. The series is noted for its detailed animation, silent narrative and interesting environments and creatures.

Bitey the Dashkin

Bitey is one of the main characters of the Brackenwood series. He resembles a hairy faced humanoid with horns and has legs that bear resemblance to a goat. He has the ability to travel great speeds over the planet and uses this to create mischief and generally be an annoyance to the other creatures of the world.
